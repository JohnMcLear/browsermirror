Browser Mirror

Browser Mirror is an exciting new way to collaborate on the web! Blah blah blah.
To use it drag this into your bookmarks, and use it to share the page you are on. (If you go to a new page you have to re-share it!)

jsmirror

How it works

Once you activate the bookmarklet your page will be sent to this server, and you will see a box hovering on the right side of the page. It has a link, "share", and anyone who goes to that link will see your session and be able to interact with it in a limited fashion.
Your sharing session will last as long as you have that page open -- if you reload or go to another page it will stop (and anyone viewing the page will see it in its last form). If you start the bookmarklet on another page then it will again be shared (at the same URL). Note that you can only share one session.
Nothing about this is secure, everything is plain text, any peer can hijack the session.
